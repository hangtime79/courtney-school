⚔ MEET THE CAST ⚔
🥇 SIR FORKSWORTH
THE FORK — brave knight with four shining tines. Guards the LEFT of the plate. Rides Noble Serviette into battle! ⚔
🐴 NOBLE SERVIETTE
THE NAPKIN — Sir Forksworth's trusty steed! Folds to the FAR LEFT of everything, furthest from the plate. The steed arrives first! 🐴
💀 SHADOW BLADE
THE KNIFE — sneaky villain with sharp blade. Blade ALWAYS faces inward toward plate. Goes on the RIGHT! 😈
💖 LADY SPOON
THE SPOON — gentle damsel who needs protecting! Hides to the RIGHT of the Knife, furthest from battle. 💕
🐴 REMEMBER: The napkin (Noble Serviette) is Sir Forksworth's horse! Placed FAR LEFT, before the fork!
🐴 STEP 1 🐴
THE NOBLE STEED ARRIVES! 🐴
I go to the
FAR LEFT! 🐴
1
Pick up the napkin 🧻
Fold it into a neat rectangle.
Place it FAR LEFT — the steed's stable!
Before any knight can charge, they need a Noble Steed! The napkin gallops to the far left — waiting to carry Sir Forksworth!
💡 Tip: The napkin is the FIRST thing placed. Far left, furthest from the plate! 🐴
⚡ STEP 2 ⚡
THE BATTLEGROUND APPEARS! 🍽️
Place plate in the
CENTER! 🍽️
Every great battle needs a battleground. The plate is the stage where all the characters take their places!
2
Pick up the plate 🍽️
Put it in the middle of the table, right in front of where you will sit.
💡 Remember: The plate never moves — everything else goes around it!
⚡ STEP 3 ⚡
THE VILLAIN LURKS! 😈
I lurk to the
RIGHT! 😈
3
Pick up the knife 🔪
Place it to the RIGHT of the plate.
Blade faces LEFT (toward the plate).
The Shadow Blade creeps RIGHT — but his sharp edge ALWAYS points toward the plate, never outward!
⚠️ Safety: Blade always faces inward — proper manners AND keeps everyone safe!
⚡ STEP 4 ⚡
SIR FORKSWORTH MOUNTS HIS STEED!
Knight & steed
guard the LEFT! ⚔️
4
Pick up the fork 🍴
Place it LEFT of the plate, beside the napkin steed.
Tines point upward.
Sir Forksworth mounts the Noble Steed and charges LEFT! Knight and steed stand together — tines raised high — guarding the battleground!
🐴⚔️ Remember: Napkin + Fork go together on the left. The steed and knight are never separated!
⚡ STEP 5 ⚡
LADY SPOON IS SAFE! 💕
Safe to the
RIGHT of knife! 💕
5
Pick up the spoon 🥄
Place it RIGHT of the knife — furthest from plate.
Round bowl faces upward.
Lady Spoon takes her safe place far right — protected by the knife between her and the battle!
💡 Tip: Knife + Spoon are both on the right. Spoon is always furthest out! 🥄
